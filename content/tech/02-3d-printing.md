I work with both FDM (fused deposition modeling) and SLA (resin) printing depending on what a project demands. FDM is great for structural parts and fast iteration, whereas SLA works well for fine detail and smooth surfaces. I model for print in Fusion 360 and Onshape, dialing in tolerances, supports, and infill settings using Cura or Bambu software, and troubleshooting failed prints along the way. 3D printing is a core part of how I prototype and fabricate custom components for art and technology projects.
