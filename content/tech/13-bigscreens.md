I produce work for some of the largest digital displays in the world, including public billboards such as those in Times Square and conference center multi-displays. I know how to design for extreme aspect ratios and non-standard resolutions, manage synchronized multi-channel video output, and use openFrameworks to build custom playback and sync systems. Big screens demand a different way of thinking about image, motion, and scale.
