I'm comfortable moving from schematic to working circuit, breadboarding, soldering, and debugging electronics for interactive and wearable projects. I work with microcontrollers, select and wire sensors, design for power constraints, and solder everything from through-hole components to surface-mount repairs. Electronics prototyping is how my physical computing and e-textile projects come alive. I also model circuits using TinkerCad, Cirkit Designer, and Fusion software.
