I use laser cutters for cutting and engraving across wood, acrylic, leather, fabric, and paper. I prepare files in Illustrator, managing cut versus engrave layers, power and speed settings, and kerf compensation for tight-fitting assemblies. Laser cutting is one of my most-used fabrication tools: precise, repeatable, and fast, and I apply it to everything from product prototyping to art installations. I have experience with Epilog, glowforge, and Trotec laser cutters.
