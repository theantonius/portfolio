I model in 3D using Autodesk Fusion 360 as my primary tool, designing parts for CNC routing, 3D printing, and laser cutting. I understand how to design for manufacturing, accounting for material tolerances, tool paths, and assembly. I also use Onshape in teaching contexts for its browser-based accessibility. CAD is how I move from an idea to something I can actually build.
