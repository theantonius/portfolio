I use Cricut and professional vinyl cutters for signage, stickers, iron-on transfers, stencils, and custom decals. I prepare files in Adobe Illustrator and on the Cricut proprietry software, handling node cleanup and path optimization to ensure clean cuts. It's a fast, versatile tool I reach for whenever a project needs sharp graphics on a physical surface and I cannot use the laser cutter for fear of burning the material. It's also great for print-then-cut practices. I have had experience with other plotters as well.
