I design and build e-textiles, garments and soft objects that incorporate electronics, sensors, and conductive materials. I sew by hand and machine, construct soft circuits using conductive thread and fabric sensors, and integrate microcontrollers into wearable form factors. The course <a href="project.html?section=education&id=talking-fabrics">Talking Fabrics</a>, which I built and taught at ITP and NYU Shanghai, grew directly out of this practice, exploring what happens when traditional craft meets programmable systems.
