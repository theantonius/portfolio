I treat curriculum design as a creative practice, building courses around hands-on making with technical skills embedded in meaningful projects. I design and teach courses in <a href="project.html?section=education&id=creative-coding-edu">creative coding</a>, <a href="project.html?section=education&id=web-dev-edu">web development</a>, <a href="project.html?section=education&id=talking-fabrics">e-textiles</a>, <a href="project.html?section=education&id=interaction-lab">physical computing</a>, and interactive media at NYU, CUNY, and NYC FIRST STEM Centers. I believe the best technical education is grounded in curiosity, and I design learning experiences that make room for experimentation and productive failure.
