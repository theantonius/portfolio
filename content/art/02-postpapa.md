Post Papa is a sketch series that I drew on Post-its while sitting beside my father in hospice.

The work was exhibited at Essex Art Center in Lawrence, MA through September 13, 2025, with half of all proceeds supporting the center, a non-profit arts community in the Merrimack Valley.
