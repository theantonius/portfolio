I operate ShopBot CNC routers for cutting, carving, and milling wood, foam, metal, polycarbonate and other sheet materials. I design in Fusion 360 through CAM toolpath generation and export g-code for machines, managing feeds, speeds, bit selection, and fixturing. CNC routing lets me work at a scale and precision that's impossible by hand, and I use it for everything from structural fabrication to decorative surface work.
